I'm a Coinbase user... how can I buy POLX?

  1. Deposit into Coinbase.com (not coinbase pro) and purchase USDT
  2. Sign up for KuCoin (KYC is not required!)
  3. In KuCoin, get your USDT wallet address under assets, "deposit", copy this address
  4. Return to Coinbase.com, and click "Send"
  5. Enter the amount, and for the "To" field, paste your KuCoin wallet address, "Pay with" should be "Tether" (USDT)
  6. Click continue and finish the process
  7. Wait for the transaction to complete (it may take a while, it is not instant)

Once you receive a deposit notification from KuCoin, you can either:

A) Use your USDT to purchase POLX
B) Use your USDT to purchase BNB, and send to a wallet (MetaMask, Trustwallet), and stake/farm using PancakeSwap and the Polylastic farming portal.

If (B) , send your BNB to your wallet, then go to PancakeSwap and swap your BNB for POLX, or LP stake for liquidity.

Once you have POLX or POLX-BNB LP tokens in your wallet, you can go to farm.polylastic.io, connect your wallet, and stake/farm your POLX!
